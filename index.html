  • To rotate the puzzle, press (ctrl + left mouse button) OR right mouse button over puzzle & drag the mouse.

    NOTE: Every puzzle has an implicit axis of rotation. Mouse movement along the horizontal direction will cause the puzzle to rotate around this axis. Mouse movement along the vertical direction will cause the puzzle to rotate around the left-right axis.

  • To twist a slice, press left mouse button over a sticker & drag the mouse.

    NOTE: for easier handling, It is recommended to keep one of the faces close to the front & use it's stickers to twist slices.

  • Select a puzzle from menu & Trigger automatic scramble.
